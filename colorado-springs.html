From Garden of the Gods to Palmer Park

Colorado Springs sits at the foot of Pikes Peak with some of the most visually dramatic outdoor event venues in the country. The terrain that makes these locations stunning is the same terrain that kills cellular signal. Fieldcast's satellite uplink works regardless of canyon walls, elevation changes, or crowd density.

Why Colorado Springs

Dramatic terrain. Reliable internet. Not usually in the same sentence.

Colorado Springs is a military city, a tourism city, and an outdoor recreation capital — which means it hosts an enormous range of outdoor events, from Pikes Peak International Hill Climb to community 5Ks in the park. The scenery is world-class. The cellular coverage in most of those venues is not.

Elevation and terrain are the core problem. Red rock formations, canyon walls, and terrain variation create cellular shadows that no amount of carrier infrastructure investment fully resolves. Fieldcast's Starlink uplink bypasses all of that — it connects directly to low-earth-orbit satellites, not ground-based towers that can't see your venue.

We serve the full Colorado Springs metro and surrounding areas, including Manitou Springs and the Ute Pass corridor. One call gets you a coverage plan within 24 hours.
