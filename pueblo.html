Pueblo outdoor venues

From the State Fair to the Reservoir

Pueblo's outdoor event scene is anchored by the Colorado State Fair — one of the largest annual events in the entire state. But it extends well beyond the fairgrounds, along the Arkansas River corridor and out to Pueblo Reservoir. Every one of these venues has the same cellular problem: designed for residents, not events.

Fairgrounds / Major Event
Colorado State Fairgrounds

The Colorado State Fair draws 500,000+ attendees over 11 days in late August. Midway vendors, livestock barns, concert pavilions, and food courts all need connected POS — and the fairgrounds' cellular towers saturate within the first hour of opening each day. Fieldcast deploys a dedicated commercial network independent of any carrier infrastructure.

Riverwalk / Downtown
Historic Arkansas Riverwalk

Pueblo's downtown waterway hosts outdoor concerts, farmers markets, the Chile & Frijoles Festival, and River Walk events year-round. Linear footprint along the canal makes for a natural Fieldcast deployment — access points elevated at key intervals, covering the full event zone from a single van position.

Reservoir / Water Sports
Pueblo Reservoir State Park

Sailing regattas, triathlon events, and outdoor festivals at the reservoir operate in a location with minimal cellular penetration. Fieldcast's Starlink uplink is purpose-built for exactly this environment — open sky, no towers, full enterprise broadband delivered from the van.

Park / Community
City Park & Mineral Palace Park

Pueblo's established municipal park system hosts community events, summer concerts, and youth sports tournaments. Fieldcast supplements the city infrastructure during high-attendance events, keeping vendor POS and registration operations fully connected throughout.

Cultural / Outdoor
Sangre de Cristo Arts Center

Outdoor events on the arts center grounds draw culturally engaged audiences who expect the same connectivity they have indoors. Fieldcast provides a premium event network — branded, managed, and scoped specifically for the event footprint.

Race / Athletic
Pueblo Motorsports Park

Track-side events, race weekends, and automotive festivals at the PMP draw enthusiastic crowds to a venue with no permanent WiFi infrastructure. Fieldcast covers timing operations, hospitality areas, and vendor zones from a single deployment.

Why Pueblo

Half a million people at the State Fair. One borrowed cell tower.

The Colorado State Fair is one of the largest events in the Rocky Mountain region — and the cellular situation at the fairgrounds during peak attendance is a well-known problem for vendors and organizers alike. Lines slow to a crawl when POS drops. Ticketing apps stop working. Emergency radio systems compete with 50,000 phones trying to upload photos.

Fieldcast was built for exactly this scenario. A completely independent uplink — satellite-based, not carrier-based — means your event's critical systems don't compete with attendee phones. POS gets guaranteed bandwidth. Staff gets a dedicated VLAN. The public gets capped but functional WiFi. And the carrier congestion that's been a problem at the fairgrounds for years is simply irrelevant to your operation.

We serve the full Pueblo metro and surrounding Pueblo County, including the Beulah corridor and the Lake Pueblo area. Same-day bookings are available for the I-25 corridor.
